t/suki is a digital makerspace for video game developers and enthusiasts to share their love and support for the game development journey.

  • forum - the webzone for yapping!
  • git - a place for your project!

you can join this community if you apply or get an invite from an existing member. t/suki is an inclusive space and welcomes game developers, vtubers, and people who enjoy games from all walks of life.

t/suki exists to build a digital alternative to in-person video game communities. you can read more here.
